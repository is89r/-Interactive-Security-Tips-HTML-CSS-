Be Careful with Links

Learn how to avoid phishing and malicious websites

Why links can be dangerous

Cybercriminals often use fake or misleading links to trick users into clicking them. These links may lead to phishing websites or download malware onto your device.

How to identify suspicious links:

  • Hover over links to preview the actual URL before clicking.
  • Look for small spelling errors or unusual domain names (like g00gle.com).
  • Don’t click links from unknown senders or pop-ups.

What to do if you're unsure:

If you receive a link and are unsure about it, do not click. You can check with the sender or use tools like VirusTotal to scan the link first.
