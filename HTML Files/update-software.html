Update Your Software

Stay secure by keeping your apps and systems up to date

Why updates matter

Software updates often fix security vulnerabilities that hackers can exploit. Ignoring updates can leave your device open to threats.

Benefits of updating regularly:

  • Fixes bugs and security holes
  • Improves performance and stability
  • Adds new features and enhancements

Best practices:

  • Enable automatic updates when possible
  • Regularly check for updates on your apps and devices
  • Only download updates from official sources
