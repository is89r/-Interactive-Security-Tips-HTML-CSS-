Use Strong Passwords

Why strong passwords matter in cybersecurity

What is a strong password?

A strong password is one that is hard to guess and not easy to crack. It usually includes a mix of upper and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.

Why is it important?

Weak passwords are one of the top causes of data breaches. Hackers often use automated tools to try common or short passwords. Using strong passwords reduces this risk.

Tips to create strong passwords:

  • Use at least 12 characters
  • Include letters, numbers, and special characters
  • Avoid names, birthdays, or repeated patterns
  • Use a password manager to generate and store passwords
